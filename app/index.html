Profile

Hi there! Welcome to nikolas-mouzourides.com. So you may be thinking; where am I? Why am I on a website which is blatantly some kind of LinkedIn profile rip off? How did I manage to spell this guy's name correctly to get onto this site in the first place? All valid questions my friend. Especially the last one.

So to answer all your questions simply, you are on my website. The truth is that in the world of today, everyone has a professional profile; all bland, barley indistinguishable and void of real personality. The purpose of this website is to break from the repetition of modern "professional" profiles and offer a more realistic view into my life, abilities and personality. A further reason is this website is a learning experience for me and a method of having fun whilst being somewhat productive.

Enjoy your stay!
